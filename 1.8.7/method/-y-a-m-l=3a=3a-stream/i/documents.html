instance method YAML::Stream#documents

documents -> Array[permalink][rdoc]

自身のドキュメントを配列で返します。 

require 'yaml'

str1=<<EOT
--- !ruby/Dog
name: pochi
---
:age: 17
:color: white
EOT

ys = YAML.load_stream(str1)
p ys.documents.pop
#=> {:age=>17, :color=>"white"}
p ys.documents.pop
#=> #<YAML::DomainType:0x2b07e24 @type_id="Dog", @domain="ruby.yaml.org,2002", @value={"name"=>"pochi"}>
p ys.documents.pop
#=> nil
